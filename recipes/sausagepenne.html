Sausage Penne

Home

  • 1 lb penne pasta
  • 8 ounces of Italian Sausage
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic
  • 1 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 lb of pasta tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Boil the penne pasta for 10 minutes
  2. Cook the sausage in a pan with garlic and olive oil
  3. Add in the tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Add in the cooked penne to the pan and mix well
